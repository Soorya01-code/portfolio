Smart Stump Vision – AI for Cricket Decisions

•Built a Machine learning system using YOLO object detection to identify the bat, foot, and ground in stumping scenarios, helping detect whether the batter is grounded or airborne at the moment of stumping. .

•Detected the distance between the foot and the ground or bat and the ground to automatically decide out or not out, minimizing reliance on human judgment and eliminating controversies in close stumping decisions.

Virtual Plane Detection System for Kabaddi

• This project introduces a virtual plane-based system in Kabaddi to accurately detect whether a player's body part (like hand, leg, or knee) has crossed the midline, which is crucial for decision-making in the game. A virtual plane is drawn at the midline to assist in detection. .

• Machine learning models are trained to recognize various body parts. When any part crosses the imaginary plane, the VR system detects it and highlights the crossed region in red. This helps avoid human error and ensures fair, real-time decisions during the match.
